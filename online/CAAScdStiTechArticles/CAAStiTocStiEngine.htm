The Application object gives access to the SmarTeam integration engine represented by the StiEngine Object, that allows to perform connection and disconnection to SmarTeam Integration, creation of SmarTeam Integration (StiDBItem) Objects and manage their lifecycle (Save, Check In, Check Out, Release, New Release, ...).

StiDBItem Objects are linked to other StiDBItem Objects than can be retrieved through the aggregated StiDBChildren object.
