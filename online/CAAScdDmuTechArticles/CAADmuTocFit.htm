A Sampled object represents the discrete specification of the trajectory of a target entity, that can be

  • a Product object,
  • a Shuttle object, which is a group of Product objects that will be moved all at once. Shuttle objects can aggregate other Shuttle objects.

This trajectory is represented by a collection of Shot objects, each Shot representing a position and how much time this position is kept.

Only one kind of Sampled object is available for now, the Track object. Track objects allow to simulate motion along the defined trajectory at a certain speed.
