Section Object

 

See Also UseCases Properties Methods

 

Sections are usefull when a user wants to understand a complex 3D assembly: a section allows visualize in a separate window a 2D extract of the 3D assembly.

Annotated views and 3D markers may be associated to a section. They are represented on the Section object by an aggregated AnnotatedViews collection and an aggregated Marked3Ds collection that allow to add, retrieve  or remove AnnotatedView objects and Marker3D objects.
