Measuring Compliance to Standards

What Should We Measure?

We measure the compliance to the rules of each component of your product:

  • The workbench
  • The views
  • The objects
  • The commands

How to Measure the Compliance to Standards?

You must fill the CATIA-DELMIA Test Matrix:

  • List all components of your product
  • Fill the non grayed cell with OK, KO, n/a.
