VPMProductSpecification Class Characteristics

name VPMProductSpecification
type Class
Role The product specification allows to instantiate a ProductRootClass inside another ProductRootClass. It is created from a ProductRootClass with possible configuration data such as specifications, ranges, or dates. A ProductSpecification is a solved product in terms of configuration.
