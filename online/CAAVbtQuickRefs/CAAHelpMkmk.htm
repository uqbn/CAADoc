With this popup you can buid modules and frameworks in your workspace.

The first time you compile your program(s) you must use the mkmk builder which will not only compile your program(s) but also generate information that will be used afterwards to accelerate compilations or to compile just one file.

After that, if IdentityCards or Imakefiles are not changed, you can just compile the current source file with "Alt+F7", or just link the corresponding module with "F7".
