The MechMod Public Specs Reference

A reference of public specs, attributes and derivability authorizations
Quick Reference

Abstract

This article describes the public contents of the MechMod catalog. It lists the StartUps and the attributes of each that are available for consulting, as well as their derivability authorizations. The "Integrating New Mechanical Features" technical article describes how to create a new startup deriving from those of the MechMod catalog and how to integrate them correctly in the application.
