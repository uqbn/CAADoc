A reference of public specs, attributes and derivability authorizations
Quick Reference

Abstract

This article describes the public contents of the AnalysisMeshingTools feature catalog. It lists the StartUps and the attributes of each that are available for consulting, as well as their derivability authorizations. The "Creating a New Analysis Features" technical article describes how to create a new startup deriving from those of the AnalysisMeshingTools catalog and how to integrate them correctly in the application.
