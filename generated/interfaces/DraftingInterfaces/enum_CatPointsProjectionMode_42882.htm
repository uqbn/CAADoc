CatPointsProjectionMode (Enumeration)

enum CatPointsProjectionMode {
  catPointsProjectionModeOff,
  catPointsProjectionModeOn

3D points projection mode.
Values:
catPointsProjectionModeOff

catPointsProjectionModeOn
