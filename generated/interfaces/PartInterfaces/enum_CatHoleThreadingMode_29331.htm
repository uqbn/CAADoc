CatHoleThreadingMode (Enumeration)

enum CatHoleThreadingMode {
  catThreadedHoleThreading,
  catSmoothHoleThreading
}
Hole threading mode.
It describes the different threadings the hole may receive.
Values:
catThreadedHoleThreading
The hole is threaded
catSmoothHoleThreading
The hole is smooth threaded
