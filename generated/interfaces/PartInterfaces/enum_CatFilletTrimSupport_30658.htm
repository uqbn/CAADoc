CatFilletTrimSupport (Enumeration)

enum CatFilletTrimSupport {
  catTrimFilletSupport,
  catNoTrimFilletSupport
}
Fillet Trim Support.
Fillets can be created on open body, so you can choose to trim of fillet support.
Values:
catTrimFilletSupport
Trim of fillet support
catNoTrimFilletSupport
No trim of fillet support
