CatHoleBottomType (Enumeration)

enum CatHoleBottomType {
  catFlatHoleBottom,
  catVHoleBottom,
  catTrimmedHoleBottom
}
Hole bottom type.
It describes the shape the hole bottom can have.
Values:
catFlatHoleBottom
The hole bottom is flat
catVHoleBottom
The hole bottom has a V-Shape
catTrimmedHoleBottom
The hole bottom has a shape obtained by trimming of hole with surface/plane
