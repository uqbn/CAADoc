CatSchIDLGRRRouteReshapeMode (Enumeration)

enum CatSchIDLGRRRouteReshapeMode {
  catSchIDLFixedShapeOff,
  catSchIDLFixedShapeOn
}
Flag for whether or not the route shape is fixed.
Role: Indicate whether or not the route shape is fixed for the purpose of reshaping the route.
Values:
catSchIDLFixedShapeOff
No need to keep the shape of the route during modification.
catSchIDLFixedShapeOn
Keep the shape of the route during modification.
