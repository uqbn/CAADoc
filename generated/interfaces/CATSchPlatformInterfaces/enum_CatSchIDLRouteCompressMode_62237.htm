CatSchIDLRouteCompressMode (Enumeration)

enum CatSchIDLRouteCompressMode {
  catSchIDLCompressOff,
  catSchIDLCompressOn
}
Flag for whether or not to compress a schematic route (i.
e., clean up its route pts). Role: Indicate whether or not to clean up the pts of a route.
Values:
catSchIDLCompressOff
Do not compress the route data.
catSchIDLCompressOn
Compress the route data.
