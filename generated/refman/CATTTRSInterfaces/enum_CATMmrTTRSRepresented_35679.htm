CATTTRSInterfaces Enumeration CATMmrTTRSRepresented

enum CATMmrTTRSRepresented {
  CATMmrTTRSRepresentedUndefined,
  CATMmrTTRSRepresentedDrived,
  CATMmrTTRSRepresentedDriving
}
Nature of the link beetwen a TTRS and it's represented TTRS.
Values:
CATMmrTTRSRepresentedUndefined
Value to be used for initialisation.
CATMmrTTRSRepresentedDrived
The represented TTRS is drived by the geometry of the current TTRS. Geometry of the represented TTRS have no influence on the TTRS Status.
CATMmrTTRSRepresentedDriving
The represented TTRS drives the geometry of the current TTRS. The status of the TTRS can be invalidated when geometry of the represented TTRS change.
