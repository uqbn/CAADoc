Mathematics Global Function CATIsign


CATIsign( iValueI,
iValueJ)
Transfers the sign of iValueJ to the CATLONG32 |iValueI|.
Returns:
The value: -CATAbs(iValueI) if iValueJ<0, CATAbs(iValueI) otherwise.
