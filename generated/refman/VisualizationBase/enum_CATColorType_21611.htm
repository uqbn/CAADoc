VisualizationBase Enumeration CATColorType

enum CATColorType {
  AmbientColor,
  DiffuseColor,
  SpecularColor,
  BlendColor
}
Color components.
Values:
AmbientColor
Affects the overall reflected light.
DiffuseColor
Affects the reflected light from light sources.
SpecularColor
Affects the visible highlights of the objects.
BlendColor
Inactive.
