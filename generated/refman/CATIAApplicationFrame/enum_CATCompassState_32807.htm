CATIAApplicationFrame Enumeration CATCompassState

enum CATCompassState {
  CatGlider,
  CatCompass
}
Possible Compass State.
Values:
CatGlider
Compass is located in the 3D scene and can manipulate objects.
CatCompass
Compass is docked on window top right corner and manipulates 3D viewpoint.
