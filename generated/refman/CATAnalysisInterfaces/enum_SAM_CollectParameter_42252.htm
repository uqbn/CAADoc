CATAnalysisInterfaces Enumeration SAM_CollectParameter

enum SAM_CollectParameter {
  Sam_CollectAdd,
  Sam_CollectReplace
}
Specify the way AnalysisEntities are collected.

Values:
Sam_CollectAdd
The collect adds the collected data on the support.
Sam_CollectReplace
The collect replaces the collected data on the support.
